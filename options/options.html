Job AutoFill

Everything you save here is stored locally in your browser and used to fill job application forms.

Behavior

You can always fill manually from the toolbar popup or the right-click menu. The extension never submits a form for you.

Address

Links

Work

Tip: {job_title} and {company} are replaced with the job title / company detected on the application page (this also works in custom answers below). If you leave the cover letter empty, a short default letter mentioning the job title and your name is used.

Education

Eligibility questions

"What level of education have you completed?" questions are answered with the Degree field from the Education section above.

Voluntary self-identification (EEO)

Leave any of these blank to skip those questions entirely.

"Sexual orientation" and "Communities" cover "select all that apply" demographic questions (Ashby and similar). Separate multiple communities with ";". When left blank, "Other" / "None of the above" are selected.

Custom questions & answers

For any other question a form asks (e.g. "Do you have experience with Python?"), add a keyword or phrase from the question on the left and your answer on the right. These take priority over the built-in fields.
